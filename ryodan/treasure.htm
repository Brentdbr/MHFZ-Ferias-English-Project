Treasure

If you have a hunting rank of 2 or higher, you can dispatch partnya to Treasure collection, which is located in the guild hall.
Match the characteristic colors when dispatching, and the higher the partnya rank, the better the rewards.

※Example of how treasure hunting works
Step 1: Join a guild with at least one other member
Step 2: Dispatch your own partnya to the guild
Step 3: Ensure that other members have dispatched their partnyas to the guild
Step 4: Attempt a guild treasure hunt in a specified locale
Step 5: Wait 30-45 minutes
There is a 33% chance to get a treasure report, if you don't receive one, repeat from step 4
Step 6: Accept treasure report
Step 7: Go to the locale you selected, pick any quest with that map
Step 8: Find blue shiny somewhere in a random area of the selected locale
Step 9: Finish the quest
Step 10: Go to the guild treasure basket
Check the basket for your treasure rewards after you have finished all of the above steps.
※Note: Once a guild dispatched partnya is used for a treasure hunt, there is a 12 hour cooldown before that cat can be used again.
With more cats dispatched to the guild from other members, you can run treasure hunts more often.

※Note that I am not confident about the classification of ranks.
