My Mission

My Mission is a facility that is unlocked at HR 5.
You can receive Missions from Secret Instructor R, or Secret Instructor S.
※However even if you complete these Missions, only the color of your medal will change.
From SR 500, so HR6 nowadays, you'll receive a Book of Secrets, and gain the following effects:

Damage ReductionReduces your damage received by (Lv÷2)%
Attack CeilingRaises your attack ceiling by Lv×40(Base:800)
Partial HPIncreases the recoverable HP bar (shown in red)
Status RecoveryReduces recovery time for status ailments by 1.5×Lv frames
